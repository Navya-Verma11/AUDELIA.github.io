Our Causes

EQUAL PAY

While individual characteristics such as education, skills or experience explain part of the gender pay gap, a large part of the gender pay gap can still be attributed purely to discrimination based on one’s gender or sex.We at Audelia help women realise their true potential and stand up for themselves for equal pay for equal work . We provide women with platforms that support gender equality in work.

“If you educate a man, you educate one person. If you educate a woman, you educate a nation.” It is extremely important to educate women in order to make them self suffient and well aware of their rights and duties. We at Audelia provide various platforms , where women with a zeal to learn and progress can easily access and explore fields of their choice.

BUSINESS

Women are as enthusiastic as their male counterparts to build profitable businesses. But many a times ,the the gender bases discrimation restricsts women to get the investors and mentors.Other problems faced by women are family responsibilities, lack of exposure, building a support network and so on. SANITATION AND HYGIENE

When women and girls are healthy, they are more likely to be able to participate in the labor force and contribute to their families and communities. Improved sanitation and hygiene can also reduce the time and resources required for healthcare, freeing up resources for other important expenses.Adequate sanitation and hygiene facilities can help to protect women's and girls' privacy and safety, particularly when it comes to managing menstruation and using the bathroom.We at Audelia, run several projects to meet the sanitation and hygiene needs of women and girls.

READ MORE >

HEALTH AND NUTRITION

Health and nutrition play a critical role in women's empowerment. Good health and nutrition not only benefit women physically but also contribute to their overall well-being and ability to participate in social, economic, and political activities. Improved health and nutrition can lead to increased productivity and improved quality of life. This can help women to participate more fully in the workforce, increasing their economic empowerment and financial independence.We at Audelia, run several projects to meet the health and nutritional needs of women and girls.

READ MORE >

MATERNAL CARE

Maternal care is an important aspect of healthcare that is crucial for the well-being of mothers and their children. Good maternal care can have a positive impact on the health and empowerment of women, especially in developing countries like India where access to quality healthcare is limited.Access to quality maternal care can improve health outcomes for both mothers and their children. This can include reducing the risk of maternal and infant mortality, as well as preventing and managing pregnancy-related complications.We at Audelia, run several projects to meet the maternal care needs of women.
